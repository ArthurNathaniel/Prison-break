About Us

"Prison Break" is a popular American television series that was first aired on the Fox network from 2005 to 2009, with a limited event series that aired in 2017. The series was created by Paul Scheuring and stars Wentworth Miller, Dominic Purcell, Amaury Nolasco, Robert Knepper, and Sarah Wayne Callies.
The show revolves around two brothers, one of whom has been sentenced to death for a crime he did not commit. The other brother, a structural engineer, devises a plan to break his sibling out of prison and clear his name. The show follows their journey as they navigate through the prison system and try to stay ahead of the authorities while uncovering a larger conspiracy.
"Prison Break" was well-received by audiences and critics, with its fast-paced action, compelling characters, and intriguing plot twists. The show has become a cult classic and has inspired a large and dedicated fan base.

Michael Scofield

Michael Scofield, portrayed by Wentworth Miller, is the main protagonist in the television series "Prison Break". Michael is a structural engineer who is desperate to save his wrongly accused brother, Lincoln Burrows, from death row.

Lincoln Burrows

Lincoln Burrows, portrayed by Dominic Purcell, is a central character in the television series "Prison Break". He is the older brother of Michael Scofield and is on death row for a crime he did not commit.

Fernando Sucre

Fernando Sucre, portrayed by Amaury Nolasco, is a character in the television series "Prison Break". Sucre is a former cellmate of Michael Scofield and Lincoln Burrows, and is depicted as a street-smart, impulsive, and somewhat unpredictable individual.
