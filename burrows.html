Role of Lincoln Burrows

Lincoln Burrows, portrayed by Dominic Purcell, is a central character in the television series "Prison Break". He is the older brother of Michael Scofield and is on death row for a crime he did not commit. Despite his difficult situation, Lincoln is depicted as a strong, determined, and resourceful individual who is always willing to stand up for what is right.

Throughout the series, Lincoln is portrayed as a supportive and caring brother who is deeply committed to his family and friends. He is often depicted as a loyal and trustworthy individual who is willing to put his own life on the line to protect those he loves. Despite his difficult circumstances, Lincoln never loses hope and remains optimistic even in the face of seemingly insurmountable obstacles.

Lincoln's role in the show is integral to the overall plot and he is depicted as a key player in the mission to break out of prison and clear his name. He is a well-developed character who is depicted as both a hero and a flawed individual, and his portrayal by Dominic Purcell is widely regarded as one of the standout elements of the series.
