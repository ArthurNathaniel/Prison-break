Role of Fernando Sucre,

Fernando Sucre, portrayed by Amaury Nolasco, is a character in the television series "Prison Break". Sucre is a former cellmate of Michael Scofield and Lincoln Burrows, and is depicted as a street-smart, impulsive, and somewhat unpredictable individual.

Despite his sometimes unpredictable behavior, Sucre is depicted as a loyal and supportive friend to Michael and Lincoln. He is often depicted as a key player in their mission to break out of prison and clear their names, and he uses his street smarts and quick thinking to help them navigate the prison system and avoid capture.

Sucre is a well-developed character who is depicted as both a comedian and a hero. Throughout the series, he provides much-needed comic relief and is a source of lightheartedness in an otherwise tense and high-stakes situation. Despite his sometimes controversial behavior, Sucre is a beloved character among "Prison Break" fans, and his performance by Amaury Nolasco is widely regarded as one of the standout elements of the series.
